About Us

Who we are

We are Uma, Ashley, Verona, and Talia. We were thinking of a community we were all part of and problems that the community faced and we chose the woman community. We chose that community because we are all girls and we can all relate to problems that the community faces. Some of the problems were Women being underrepresented in the work place and people who lack proper SexEd making decisions of our bodies, and we thought by making a discussion board, blog, and explaining how SexEd is important, and putting a few charities that you can donate to, would help our cause, bring awarness, and make a better future.
